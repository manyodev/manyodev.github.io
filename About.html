What is SKZPD?

This is a fan-made website that I (Manyo) created to act as a resource for collecting Stray Kids photocards.

This site is a work in progress, so look forward to improvements in quality & functionality!

Update Log

Note: The plan is to have KARMA 100% updated before DO IT. Then to have DO IT 100% updated before working to update other content. The same for every future comeback. Meanwhile photocards for previous releases will be updated during downtime between comebacks. It may make updates for older content slower, but make it easier to not fall behind on new content.
